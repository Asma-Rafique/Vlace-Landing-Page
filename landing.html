About us

Quality Meat from Farm to Table

At Vlace, we're passionate about connecting people with premium quality bulk beef and pork sourced locally from small family farms and butcher shops. In doing so - we will unveil the source of our food so the customer knows where it came from, just how it used to be.

Our platform was created to share our passion for a superior way to buy meat—locally and in bulk. This approach offers a convenient, high-quality, and valuable source of protein
